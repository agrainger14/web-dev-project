Mood Score

The score is rated from 0 - 100, a score closer to 0 represents that you have logged more negative moods while a score closer to 100 means you have logged more positive moods.

    Mood Count

    An interactive chart which provides a visual display of all the ratings from your recorded moods. The value centered within the chart displays a total of all logs recorded and the colour coded legends provides a visual of the different values.

    The line chart is a visualisation of the latest 25 mood logs recorded across the labelled dates and times.

    Areas in green represent a period in which your felt more positive and red areas represent negative entries.
