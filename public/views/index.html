Welcome to MoodRec

An online mood tracking web-journal.

Mood journaling involves recording your mood over periods of time so that you can recognise trends and potential triggers. Once these trends are recognised, you can work to eliminate and avoid certain triggers and focus your energy on how best to respond. Journaling your feelings, emotions and challenges has been shown to reduce anxiety and depression.

After logging into MoodRec, the website features will become available. Members are able to utilise the features to log a mood entry, view previous recorded entries or a visual mood summary.

Mood Log

A mood log session will begin and a visual slider will be displayed to help represent how your currently feeling. Additional context into your mood can also be entered and if needed a pre-populated list of moods can also be chosen.

Mood Log

Mood List

View a list of all your previously recorded mood logs that are stored within the database. You also will be given the option to update the context of previous recorded mood logs if required, or delete previously recorded entries.

Mood List

Mood Summary

A summary of your recorded moods will be displayed to provide a visual representation and an analysis of your mood logs. A mood score, mood count and trending chart will be generated for the user.
